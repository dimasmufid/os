# Phase 4 — AI + Social Layer (Growth & Community)

## Goal
Enhance long-term engagement, personalization, and community connection through AI systems and multiplayer/social features.

---

# 1. Core Features

## 1.1 AI Quest Generator
AI creates quests based on:
- User’s goals
- Past behavior
- Streak patterns
- Skill areas

Examples:
- “Complete 3 study missions this week”
- “Work on Altiera for 2 days”
- “Do 2 training sessions for health”

## 1.2 Personal AI Coach
- Daily reflections
- Suggest improvements
- Motivate streak maintenance
- Provide clarity on goals

## 1.3 Multiplayer Plaza
- See other users as avatars
- Real-time presence
- Chat bubbles
- Simple gestures / emotes

## 1.4 Guild Rooms / Group Spaces
- Create/join guilds
- Group missions
- Shared world spaces

## 1.5 Cloud Sync (Paid)
User’s devices share:
- World state
- Inventory
- Hero progression
- Sessions

## 1.6 Marketplace (Paid)
User-generated content:
- Tilesets
- World themes
- Cosmetics
- NPC packs

---

# 2. Deliverables
- AI agent (OpenAI / custom backend)
- Presence WebSocket layer
- Multiplayer world scene
- Guild backend
- Cloud sync storage
- Marketplace architecture

---

# 3. Success Criteria
- Users feel guided and supported
- Strong community interactions
- Network effects begin
- Monetization paths open naturally
